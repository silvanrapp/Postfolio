# Postfolio ●

**Carousels & stories, in seconds.**
No Photoshop. No login. No excuses.

**Live:** [silvanrapp.github.io/Carousel-Builder](https://silvanrapp.github.io/Carousel-Builder/)

![Postfolio — carousels & stories, in seconds](og-image.png)

## What it does

Postfolio is a carousel builder for Instagram — made by a photographer, for photo lovers. Drop in your photos, arrange, frame, export. Runs entirely in your browser on iPhone and desktop. Your photos never leave your device.

## Why it exists

5 pm again. Posting time is at 6. Great photos everywhere — and still no post ready, because building a carousel used to mean opening Photoshop. Postfolio removes that hurdle: open the app, drop a photo, done.

## Highlights

- **Frames** — the editorial look that's everywhere right now: framed photos that quote each other
- **Panorama split** — one wide photo, seamless across 2+ slides
- **Magazine typography** — cover-style text slides, colors picked straight from your photo
- **Free layout** — drag, zoom, rotate, smart-snap guides, crop with ratio presets
- **Export-ready** — Post (4:5, 1080×1350) & Story (9:16, 1080×1920) as PNG, ZIP, or straight to Photos on iPhone
- **Zero friction** — free, no login, runs locally in the browser
