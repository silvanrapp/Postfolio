# Carousel Creator

A browser-based tool for building multi-slide Instagram carousels and Stories — drag, zoom, rotate, smart-snap, and a built-in text-overlay editor, all running locally in the browser. No app, no login.

**Live:** _add link once published, e.g. silvanrapp.github.io/carousel-creator_

## How it works

Add slides, drop in photos or styled text boxes, and drag/zoom/rotate them into place — smart guides snap to centers, edges, other photos, and equal spacing automatically. Switch between Post (4:5) and Story (9:16) format with one click, then export every slide as PNG, bundled as a .zip, or shared straight to Photos on iPhone.

## Features

- Tap, drag & drop to load photos; add text boxes with 5 fonts, 3 weights (Light/Regular/Semibold), bold/italic/underline, and 5 colors
- Multi-slide carousels with smart snap guides (center, edge, alignment, equal spacing)
- Drag corner handles to resize, the top handle to rotate, or pinch with two fingers on touch devices
- Frame borders (3 sizes) with one-tap auto-fit into the slide or a custom layout
- Row/column split presets for stacked or side-by-side photo layouts
- Crop tool with rule-of-thirds grid and aspect-ratio presets (1:1, 4:5, 5:4, 2:3, 3:2, full slide)
- Zoom controls to work close-up or fit all slides into view at once
- Export all slides as PNG, download all as a .zip, or save all to Photos via the iOS share sheet
- Two formats: Post (4:5, 1080×1350) and Story (9:16, 1080×1920)
- Works on iPhone and desktop
