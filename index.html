Postfolio · Help

Good to know.

Getting started▾
  • Tap the empty slide to add a photo — or pick several at once: each lands on its own slide.
  • “+” in the filmstrip adds a slide. Long-press a tile to reorder.
  • The duplicate button (bottom right) copies a slide with all its style — then just replace the photo.
  • Every photo you add is kept in your Library — reuse it anytime without re-importing (Library button in the toolbar).
Gestures▾
  • Tap once to select — only the selected element can be moved, so nothing ever shifts by accident. Tap ✓ (bottom right) when you're done.
  • Double-tap a photo to crop and choose the visible section.
  • Pinch to zoom a photo inside its frame.
  • Drag the red dot to resize — it snaps to slide edges and other photos.
  • Swipe between slides — the active one always snaps to center.
Frames & panorama▾
  • Frames: one photo big, one small in a white frame. Fill a background and the small frame of the next band fills automatically — the classic look: two photos, four fields.
  • Split-panorama (in Crop): one wide photo runs seamlessly across 2–3 slides. Instagram shows it as one continuous image when swiping.
  • Mirror (in frames and 2–3-photo templates): the paired photos move symmetrically — turn it off to place each one freely.
Frame & style▾
  • Border draws a frame right around the photo. Margin adds space to the slide edge in the background colour — together they make a clean double frame.
  • Blur softens a photo — put a blurred copy behind a sharp, smaller one for a soft frosted-glass frame.
Your projects▾
  • Everything is saved locally on your device — nothing leaves it.
  • Your projects live only on this device — export finished carousels to keep them safe.
  • Long-press a project on the start screen to move, duplicate or delete it.

Tips

  • iPhone: Share → “Add to Home Screen” — Postfolio runs like a real app, fullscreen without browser bars.
  • Export “All slides” gives you numbered images, ready to upload in order.
  • Add the outro slide to any carousel — it credits the app and looks good doing it ♥
